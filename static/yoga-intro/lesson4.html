Introduction

Principles

  • When seated on the floor, keep your spine straight. Use props if this is challenging.
  • As you bend forward, continue lengthening your spine.
  • Use the breath to deepen a pose.

Benefits

These poses can be practiced daily. With consistent practice, they will enable you to sit on the floor for a long time and will improve circulation in your lower extremities.

Props

Yoga mat. A blanket or a meditation cushion if you can't sit upright.

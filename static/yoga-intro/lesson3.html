"Breath is central to yoga because it is central to life ...and yoga is about life."—Sri T. Krishnamacharya

Introduction

Principles

  • Let your breath become slow, smooth, soft and rhythmic.
  • Keep your face, shoulders, chest, and abdomen relaxed.
  • If you are getting dizzy, you are trying too hard! Rest and take it a little more slowly next time.

Benefits

Ujjayi breath has a calming, restorative effect on the nervous system. It enhances your yoga practice, creating a union between mind and body. Please make sure to maintain ujjayi breath throughout other lessons.

Props

Yoga mat. A blanket under your head if your chin juts out while lying on your back.
